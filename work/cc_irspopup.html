What I did...

In 2000 I did maintenance on this ASP(VBScript) intranet site. I improved the organization/readability of existing pages, developed many new sub-pages, and standardized the "look and feel" of the whole site. I also added many pdf files to improve printability of commonly printed forms, tables, and letters. I wrote and added tools to this site, such as the document manager and the link manager, and I set-up the search engine using index server.

This is an Intranet site and so can not be viewed on-line.
