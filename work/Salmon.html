What I did...

Working solo as a contractor at the VA office between 2000 and 2003, I was tasked with developing a dynamic web portal, and accompanying content management system. Overtime, I enchanced the portal tool making it customizable so that any group or organization could use it, very similar to a "Share Point" Portal. All content resides in a database, and can be updated via the CMS tool. The CMS tool also allows layout to be changed, and new sections to be added.
